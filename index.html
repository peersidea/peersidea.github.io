Have you ever tried to learn a hobby online? Are all the teachers you've looked for overqualified for your needs?

With PeersIdea, our goal is to provide hobby learned with personalized and affordable teachers. While most online sites focus on academic topics such as Biology, or the SATs, PeersIdea is commited to uniting enthusiasts of various hobbies, from chess, to knitting, to sleighing. Anyone can be a teacher, status is based on peer reviews. This process ensures that anyone has the opportunity to teach, and everyone has the opportunity to learn.
